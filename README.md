# 🎯 Perfect 10

A deceptively simple browser game that challenges your sense of time.

## 🌐 Play Now

https://perfect-10.netlify.app

---

## 🎮 The Challenge

Can you stop the timer at **exactly 10.00 seconds**?

Sounds easy.

It isn't.

Start the timer, rely on your internal clock, and try to hit the perfect score. Every hundredth of a second matters.

## 📸 Gameplay

### Objective

Stop the timer at:

```text
10.00 seconds
```

The closer you get, the better your score.

### Examples

```text
10.00 = PERFECT 🎉

9.99 = So Close 😭

10.01 = Almost There 😬

9.52 = Keep Practicing 😅
```

---

## 🚀 Why I Built This

Most people think they can accurately estimate 10 seconds.

This game proves otherwise.

Perfect 10 is a tiny experiment in human perception, reaction time, and just a little bit of frustration.
